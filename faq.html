Frequently Asked Questions

1. Who can participate in the Mathematics Olympiad?
Students studying in Class 8th to Class 12th from any recognized school are eligible to participate.
2. Is there any syllabus for the Olympiad?
Yes, you can find the syllabus on the official website of IITB Mathematics Olympiad.
3. What is the mode of the examination?
The examination will be conducted in Offline mode at the IIT Bombay campus.
4. When is the exam date?
The exam is on 8th February, 2026.
5. What is the exam duration and timings?
Duration: 3 hours | Timing: 10am - 1pm
6. Is there any registration fee?
Yes: ₹399 + Platform charges.
Note: This fee is non-refundable.
7. How do I complete my registration?
Fill in the registration form and complete the payment. A confirmation message/email will be sent after successful registration.
8. What is the last date to register?
The last date for registration is 1st February 2026. Late registrations will not be accepted.
9. Will there be any on-spot registration?
There will be no on-spot registration this year.
10. Will students receive a certificate?
Yes, all participants will receive a Certificate of Participation. Merit certificates and awards will be given to top performers.
11. Are there any prizes for the top performances?
Top performers will receive Certificates of Excellence and Cash Prizes.
12. What if I make a mistake in the registration form?
You can contact the organizing team before the registration deadline for corrections.
13. Does this guarantee a seat in IIT Bombay?
No.
14. Whom should I contact for queries?
Email: olympiad@math.iitb.ac.in
